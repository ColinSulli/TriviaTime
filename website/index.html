Music Maker is an interactive website where users can create their own music or “beats” through keyboard and mouse input. After each keypress or click, the site will play a different tone/sound/etc. and display a different image/animation on the screen. The user will have the ability to create a musical animation of their choice, using the different interactive capabilities that this website affords.

The landing screen is, by default, on Random mode. You will be presented a single instruction: Press any alphanumeric key to start.
Keys [0-9] and [A-Z] will each generate a unique sound. Each mode has a different bank of sounds to draw from. Note that random mode will generate a different sound for the same key stroke. Using your mouse, you can switch to different "modes" and soundboards, such as Techno, Drums, and Piano.
The final option is the Tutorial mode, which will walk you through how to play Twinkle Twinkle Little Star. Note that although you can press the keys at your own will, the tutorial walkthrough does not formally begin until after you press the Start button.
